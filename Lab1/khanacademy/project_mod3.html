Recipe Pancakes

  • Time: 45
  • Serves: 2-3 portions
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
baking powder 2 1/2 teaspoons
granulated sugar 3 tablespoons
salt 1/2 teaspoon
eggs 2
milk 1 1/2 cups
melted butter 2 tablespoons
oil

Step 1: Gather the ingredients.

Step 2: In a bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt. Whisk or stir to blend thoroughly.

Step 3: In another bowl, whisk together the eggs and milk.

Step 4: Add the egg-milk mixture to the flour mixture, stirring just until blended.

Step 5: Mix in the melted butter. If the batter seems too thick to spread, add a little more milk.

Step 6: Using about 1/4 cup of batter for each pancake, drop onto a hot, greased griddle.

Step 7: Cook until you see that the bubbles over the surface are breaking and the edges appear somewhat dry.

Step 8: Turn to brown the other side and finish cooking.

Recipe Chocolate milkshake

  • Time:10
  • Serves:1
Ingredients Quantity
chocolate hazelnut spread 1 tbsp
semi-skimmed milk 250ml
chocolate ice creams 2 scoops
marshmallows 5
double cream 30ml

Step 1: Load the back of a teaspoon with a little of the chocolate spread and spread it in a long line from the bottom of the inside of the glass to the top. Put the rest of the chocolate spread into a bowl in the microwave or in a small saucepan over a low heat. Warm through for a few seconds just until runny and set aside.

Step 2:Put the milk and chocolate ice cream into a blender. Whizz everything up until smooth then pour into your prepared glass.

Step 3:Top with a layer of the marshmallows and a spoonful of whipped cream. Decorate with a drizzle of the warmed chocolate spread. Serve immediately with a straw.
